About Reynanda

We're proud to present Reynanda Inc., an up-and-coming power supply business based out of Edmonton, AB. Our mission is to provide reliable power supplies to our customers, and we strive to meet that goal every day. We focus on quality products, customer service, and innovation.We offer a wide range of power supplies, from small, low-voltage AC/DC converters to large industrial power supplies. We also stock a variety of specialty items such as batteries, cable, and connectors. Our team is experienced in all aspects of power supply, so we can help you find the perfect solution for your needs. We are dedicated to providing the best service and products to the Edmonton area and beyond.
